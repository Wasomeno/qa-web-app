# Product

## Register

product

## Users

QA engineers who need to manage test coverage, track issues, and validate software quality. They work under time pressure during release cycles and need to quickly assess test status, review recordings, and triage failures. They value precision, clarity, and speed in their tools.

## Product Purpose

A quality assurance platform that helps QA engineers manage test scenarios, recordings, issues, and AI-assisted debugging. Success means users feel confident in their test coverage and in control of the quality process, with minimal friction between intent and action.

## Brand Personality

Sleek, efficient, modern. The interface should feel like a precision instrument, not a burden. Every element earns its place.

## References

- **Linear:** Speed and polish, keyboard-first interactions, crisp typography, generous whitespace
- **Notion:** Clarity of hierarchy, clean content areas, thoughtful empty states
- **GitLab:** Information density when needed, familiar patterns for issue/project management

## Anti-references

Avoid clutter. No unnecessary decoration, no visual noise that distracts from the task. Every pixel should serve the user's goal.

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over abundance.** Show what matters, hide what doesn't. A tool that respects attention.

2. **Speed is a feature.** Fast interactions, instant feedback, no waiting. The interface should feel immediate.

3. **Confidence through clarity.** Users should always know where they are, what's happening, and what they can do next.

4. **Density with purpose.** When information density is needed, it's structured and scannable, not overwhelming.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard web accessibility practices. No specific requirements noted, but maintain sufficient color contrast and keyboard navigability as baseline quality.
